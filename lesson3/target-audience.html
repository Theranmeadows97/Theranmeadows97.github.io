Target Audience

Description

My target audience is the people living in the south Idaho area. My target audience age is between 20 and 50 years of age who may have an interest in knowing the weather. I would like them to know what the weather is for the day and possible recommendations or precautions.

Personas

1)Persona 1:
James Waters

Forest Ranger, Green Woods
Age 45

James Waters relies heavily on knowing weather conditions due to his job as a Forest Ranger. He personally wants to know the weather for the safety of his family and to others he will direct along in traffic.

2) Persona 2:
Martha Fires

Social Worker, Found Family
Age 34

Martha has a 30-minute commute to her work and must cross a narrow bridge. She will need to know about the condition of the weather, streets, and accidents if any on the bridge.

Scenarios

1) 1) James notices the clouds turning black and wants to know how many inches is expected to rain.

2) 2) It is a cold winter day that snowed 2 inched the previous night. Martha wants to know if the roads are too icy to drive.

3)3) James is having a day off and wants to go on a hike with his family. He wants to know some recommendations on clothes he should were based on the weather.

4) 4) Martha is stuck in traffic and wants to know if there was an accident and approximately how long it will be to pass.

5) 5) James is working in the middle of the woods and wants to see a satellite image of the forest overcast.
