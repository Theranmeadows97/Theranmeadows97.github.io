About the Chamber

The mission of White Tank Mountain Commerce in Waddell is to achieve economic prosperity, develop a pro-business community, and to improve the quality of life in our region.

We invite anyone in the community that seeks to better their neighborhood and be involved in the decisions that affect our future to join our local membership!
