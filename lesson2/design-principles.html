Design Principles

Visual Hierarchy

Love Book Online

Love Book Online

Love Book Online shows the principal of visual hierarchy. You can see that just below the books, the words start in a bigger font and get smaller leading you down to a button to "Get Started Now". Scrolling down on the mobile site, you can continue to see the same principal applied. They used this principal to attract you to other offers and services that make it easier for you to complete.

White Space

Threadless

Threadless

Threadless shows the principal of white space. You can see that the background is white and when it is coupled with the images that take up the entire width of the viewport. The spacing of the images and the contrast of the background colors give the site a nice and clean straight edge feel. It also helps that the proximity of the content within the boxes helps to know what each square is about.

Contrast

Random Scriptutre Generator

Random Scripture Generator

The Random Scripture Generator site is very simple. The colors it sefl may be a bit off, but it helps with the contrast that each section is a different color. The color is consistent through the site so you know what material and book you are looking from.
